History of the area

1830 John Rodman petitioned the US and Spain for 166,000
acres of land. He used this land for the timber. This land was then bought and sold from 1892 to 1954.

1892 this land and surrounding land was bought by John J Cummings where he continued the lumber yards and naval store operations.

The land prospered for many years while the railroad ran through the town.
Once it closed the land was sold off. The land was bought by the Tilton family and turned into a ranch till the mid 70s.

In the mid 70s the land was bought and sold many times till the 90s where it was bought up by the current owners.

Myth

From the early days the area grew with population. The main food source for these people were the wild hogs. The hog population was high and caused issues with the local loggers.

There was lore about a monstrous Hog that tormented the farmers and loggers in the community. Over time lore started about this Hog and how it became the hunter.

It is said that on a cold quiet night peope can hear the strange sounds of a hog in the distance. Is that Monstrous Hog still out there hunting???
